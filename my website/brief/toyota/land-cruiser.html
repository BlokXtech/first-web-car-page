Land Cruiser 300

The latest generation Land Cruiser 300 combines luxury with unmatched off-road capability, featuring advanced technology and powerful performance.

  • Full-Time 4WD with locking differentials
  • Multi-Terrain Select system

Land Cruiser Heritage Edition

The Heritage Edition pays homage to the Land Cruiser's legendary history with retro styling cues and enhanced off-road features.

Features:

  • 5.7L V8 engine (381hp)
  • KDSS suspension system
  • Heritage badging and unique exterior
  • Roof rack and additional storage
