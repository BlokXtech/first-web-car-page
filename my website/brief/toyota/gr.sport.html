GR Corolla

The GR Corolla brings rally-inspired performance to the compact segment with its turbocharged 3-cylinder engine and advanced GR-FOUR all-wheel drive system.

GR Supra

The legendary Supra returns with BMW-sourced turbocharged power and Toyota's legendary tuning expertise, creating the ultimate sports coupe.

Key Features:

  • 3.0L Turbocharged I6 (382hp)
  • Rear-wheel drive
  • 8-speed automatic transmission
  • Active differential
