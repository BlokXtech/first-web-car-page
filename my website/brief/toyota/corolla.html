Corolla Sedan

The Toyota Corolla Sedan offers a perfect blend of comfort, efficiency, and modern technology for everyday driving.

The sporty Corolla Hatchback combines dynamic styling with practical cargo space for a more engaging drive.

Key Features:

  • 2.0L Dynamic Force engine (168hp)
  • 6-speed manual or CVT
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • 18-inch alloy wheels
